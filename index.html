Events

9th April 2023 (First half)

Godh Bharai and Ring Ceremony 11AM - 1PM

Engagement ceremony or Sagaai has become synonymous with the ring ceremony. Traditionally, Sagaai involved both the families exchanging gifts and presenting bride to be with beautiful jewellery, her dresses and much more. Together this is known as Godh Bharai. But for our part, its putting the ring into each other's fingers and blasting the champagne out of its bottle.

Cake cutting Ceremony 1:00PM - 1:30PM

Right after we've exchanged the rings and taken the blessings of the elders, its time to cut the cake in celebration. Don't worry, we will make sure to sweeten your mouth with a good quality cake.

9th April 2023 (Second half)

Dance Performances 1:30PM - 2:00PM

What else could be more fun then seeing some beautiful dance performances while you enjoy your snacks. Get the hang of the event by some amazing music followed by some amazing performance. This will give you a teaser of the Sangeet ceremony through our dance routine.

Lunch and some chit-chat 2PM - 4PM

This is the time to interact and have some gup-shups about anything and everything. Now, is the time to have a full fledged lunch and to cherish your stomach as well. Or, if you wish, you can show your dance moves too over the DJ.
